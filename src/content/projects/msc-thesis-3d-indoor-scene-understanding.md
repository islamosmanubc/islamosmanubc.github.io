## Problem

Indoor scene understanding requires reasoning about objects, geometry, and temporal consistency across image sequences. RGB-D data adds structure, but it also introduces its own sensing and modeling challenges.

## Role

My MSc research explored how detection, tracking, and segmentation can be combined to produce a more structured understanding of indoor scenes.

## Approach

- Studied object detection, tracking, and segmentation for RGB-D sequences.
- Compared feature and descriptor choices for indoor-scene interpretation.
- Focused on spatial structure and temporal consistency rather than only single-frame classification.

## Tools

RGB-D computer vision, scene understanding, object tracking, and segmentation research.

## Outcome

This work laid the foundation for later research in foreground segmentation, domain generalization, and broader video understanding.
